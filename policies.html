We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both parties, considering the commitments we make to our service providers on your behalf.

Special Considerations

Flight Tickets

Airline cancellation policies vary and are separate from our policy. Most discounted airfares are 100% non-refundable once ticketed.

Hotel Bookings

Many hotels have their own cancellation policies, especially during peak seasons. Some may charge 100% for last-minute cancellations.

Visa Fees

Visa application fees, documentation charges, and courier costs are 100% non-refundable once the process has begun.

Force Majeure Clause

In case of circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, political unrest, pandemics, government restrictions, etc.):

  • We will work with service providers to obtain maximum possible refunds
  • Credit notes may be issued instead of cash refunds
  • Administrative fees may apply for processing
  • Travel insurance claims should be filed by the traveler

Travel Insurance Recommended: We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance that covers trip cancellation.

Need to Cancel Your Trip?

If you need to cancel your booking, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to cancellations@jambudviptours.com with your booking reference
  2. Clearly state your cancellation request and reason
  3. We'll respond within 24 hours with applicable charges
  4. Upon agreement, we'll process your refund within 7-10 working days

General Terms & Conditions

These terms and conditions govern the relationship between Jambudvip Tours & Travels (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") and the client (hereinafter referred to as "the Traveler"). By booking with us, you agree to these terms.

1. Tour Inclusions & Exclusions

What's Included:
  • Accommodation as specified in the itinerary
  • Meals as mentioned (B=Breakfast, L=Lunch, D=Dinner)
  • Transportation as per itinerary
  • Sightseeing and activities mentioned in inclusions
  • Services of guides/drivers as specified
  • All currently applicable taxes
What's Not Included:
  • Any airfare, unless specifically mentioned
  • Any meals other than those specified
  • Personal expenses such as laundry, telephone calls, tips, etc.
  • Any additional tours/excursions not mentioned in itinerary
  • Travel insurance
  • Visa fees and documentation charges
  • Anything not specifically mentioned under "inclusions"

2. Customer Responsibilities

The Traveler agrees to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information during booking
  • Carry valid identity proof and travel documents
  • Adhere to the tour schedule and timings
  • Follow local laws, customs, and regulations
  • Respect cultural sensitivities at destinations
  • Inform the Company of any special needs/medical conditions
  • Purchase adequate travel insurance
  • Make payments as per the agreed schedule

3. Liability Limitations

The Company acts as an intermediary between the Traveler and various service providers (hotels, airlines, transport companies, etc.). The Company shall not be responsible for:

  • Any loss, injury, or damage to person or property
  • Additional expenses due to delays, accidents, or natural disasters
  • Changes in itinerary due to circumstances beyond our control
  • Loss of baggage or personal belongings
  • Acts or omissions of any third-party service providers
  • Any inconvenience caused due to political disturbances, natural calamities, or technical problems

The Company's liability in any case shall not exceed the total amount paid for the tour.

4. Dispute Resolution

In case of any dispute:

  • The Traveler must first contact the Company in writing
  • Both parties agree to attempt amicable resolution
  • If unresolved, disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in [City], India
  • Indian law shall govern these terms and conditions

We believe in fair resolution and will make every effort to address concerns promptly and fairly.

5. Changes & Amendments

The Company reserves the right to:

  • Make minor changes to the itinerary when necessary
  • Substitute accommodation with similar category hotels
  • Change transportation modes if required
  • Alter the order of sightseeing if necessary

Major changes will be communicated to the Traveler and will require mutual agreement.

Amendment Fees:
  • Date changes (if possible): ₹2,000 per person + any supplier charges
  • Name changes: ₹1,000 per person + any airline/hotel charges
  • Itinerary changes after confirmation: ₹3,000 + any additional costs

Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your personal information

Our Commitment to Privacy

Jambudvip Tours & Travels is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:
  • Name, contact details (phone, email, address)
  • Date of birth, gender, nationality
  • Passport and visa information
  • Payment information
  • Travel preferences and special requirements
Automatically Collected Information:
  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Website usage data through cookies
  • Location data (with your permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process bookings and provide requested services
  • Communicate about your travel arrangements
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely for providing and improving our travel services.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Hotels, airlines, transport companies, guides, and activity providers necessary for your travel
  • Government Authorities: When required by law or for visa processing
  • Business Partners: Only with your explicit consent for additional services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
Third-Party Agreements:

All our service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for providing the contracted services.

4. Data Protection Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

SSL encryption for all data transmission

Access Control

Limited access to authorized personnel only

Secure Storage

Protected servers with regular security audits

Employee Training

Regular privacy and security training

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request information about data sharing
How to Exercise Your Rights:

Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@jambudviptours.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • Booking Information: 7 years for legal and tax purposes
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Inactive Accounts: 3 years, then anonymized
  • Financial Records: 10 years as per Indian law

After retention periods, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Provide personalized content
Cookie Control:

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.
