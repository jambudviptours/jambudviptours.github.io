Making Travel Accessible & Memorable

At Jambudvip Tours & Travels, we believe that travel has the power to transform lives, broaden perspectives, and create lifelong memories. Founded in 2022, our mission is simple: to make exceptional travel experiences accessible to everyone, regardless of background, religion, or travel history.

We go beyond booking tickets and hotels. We craft journeys that resonate with your soul, create itineraries that tell your story, and provide support that makes you feel at home anywhere in the world.

Meet Our Founder

Rahul Jagtap

Founder & Chief Travel Consultant

"I started Jambudvip with a simple vision: to create travel experiences that transcend boundaries - not just geographical, but also those of religion, background, and perceived limitations. Travel should unite us, not divide us."

With over a decade of experience in the travel industry, Rahul noticed a gap in the market. Many travelers felt excluded or misunderstood by traditional travel agencies. Some hesitated to explore certain destinations due to cultural or religious concerns. Others felt their unique preferences weren't being heard.

This inspired Rahul to establish Jambudvip Tours & Travels in 2022. The name "Jambudvip" comes from ancient Indian geography, representing the entire inhabited world - a fitting symbol for our inclusive approach to travel.

Rahul personally oversees the training of all travel consultants, ensuring they understand the nuances of different cultures, dietary requirements, and religious sensitivities. His hands-on approach has helped build a team that truly understands the art of personalized travel.
